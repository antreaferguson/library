5-5-5 Reset

This quick breathing technique helps calm your nervous system when you're feeling anxious or overwhelmed.

01:00

Prepare to begin the 5-5-5 Reset:

1. Inhale for 5 seconds

2. Hold for 5 seconds

3. Exhale for 5 seconds

4. Repeat for 1 minute

Body Grounding Scan

This sensory awareness practice brings you back to the present moment when you're feeling anxious or reactive.

02:00

Prepare to begin the Body Grounding Scan:

1. Notice 5 things you can see

2. Notice 4 things you can touch

3. Notice 3 things you can hear

4. Notice 2 things you can smell

5. Notice 1 thing you can taste

Vagus Nerve Reset

This physical technique directly activates your parasympathetic nervous system to reduce anxiety and reactivity.

00:30

Prepare to begin the Vagus Nerve Reset:

1. Take a deep breath in

2. Hum on the exhale

3. Feel the vibration in your chest

4. Continue for 30 seconds

Breath of Fire

This energizing breath technique increases alertness and focus when you're feeling foggy or disconnected.

01:00

Prepare to begin Breath of Fire:

1. Sit with a straight spine

2. Take a deep breath in

3. Begin rapid, rhythmic breathing through your nose

4. Focus on sharp, powerful exhales

5. Continue for 1 minute

Cross-Body Activation

These movement patterns integrate both hemispheres of your brain to improve mental clarity.

02:00

Prepare to begin Cross-Body Activation:

1. Touch your right hand to your left knee

2. Touch your left hand to your right knee

3. Continue alternating sides

4. Move at a comfortable pace for 2 minutes

Cold Exposure

This quick temperature change resets your alertness when you're feeling foggy or disconnected.

00:30

Prepare to begin Cold Exposure:

1. Splash cold water on your face

2. Or place a cold cloth on the back of your neck

3. Focus on the sensation for 30 seconds

4. Take deep breaths as you experience the cold

Coherent Breathing

This breathing pattern balances your autonomic nervous system for optimal decision-making.

03:00

Prepare to begin Coherent Breathing:

1. Inhale for 5.5 seconds

2. Exhale for 5.5 seconds

3. Continue this rhythm

4. Practice for 3 minutes

3-3-6 Breath

Prepare to begin 3-3-6 Breath:

3. Exhale for 6 seconds

Body Tapping Sequence

This gentle tapping routine regulates energy throughout your body for balanced decision-making.

01:00

Prepare to begin Body Tapping Sequence:

1. Use your fingertips to gently tap the top of your head

2. Move to your forehead, then temples

3. Tap under your eyes, then collarbone

4. Finish by tapping the sides of your ribs

5. Spend about 10 seconds on each area
